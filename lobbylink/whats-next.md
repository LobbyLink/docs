# What's next?

Now that you initialized your WebRTC connection you can get startet on sending Data from peer to peer and initialize multiplayer instances. For that Godot has the nodes [MultiplayerSpawner](https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/classes/class_multiplayerspawner.html), [MultiplayerSynchronizer](https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/classes/class_multiplayersynchronizer.html), the [MultiplayerAPI](https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/classes/class_multiplayerapi.html) and of course [RPCs](https://docs.godotengine.org/en/stable/tutorials/networking/high_level_multiplayer.html#remote-procedure-calls) (Remote Procedure Calls). Maybe I will extend this guide in the future to be more multiplayer focused, after the connection is established but that will be in the far future.
